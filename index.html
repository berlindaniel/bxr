Welcome!

This is the homepage for team BXR, a group of Undergrad CS students at Duke working with the early-stage startup for CS290 - Software Rapid Prototyping. This webpage will serve as a source to document our work and progress throughout the semester.

A brief overview of BXR...

BXR is a company that connects AirBnb users to consumer products. Brands will advertise their products by letting AirBnb customers sample them in a home setting. Cutomers will leave detailed reviews on the products in exchange for some sort of reward (money, points). Brands will benefit from increased exposure and user testing information. Our goal is to provide BXR with a static website and QR code survey functionality for user reviews. Time-permitting, we could explore mobile design in iOS.

Roadmap

  • Stage 1 (2/11 to 3/2)
    1. Web Development: Deliver a static homepage for BXR to give an overview of the company and its mission
    2. Branding: Assist BXR founders with branding and logo design focusing on keywords and values surrounding BXR
    3. QR/Survey: Basic scanning and surveying, utilizing Google Forms and built-in QR phone scanners
  • Stage 2 (3/2 to 3/25)
    1. Web Development: Add additional functionalities, pages, and information to website. Explore potential of merging surveying/database techniques to website
  • Stage 3 (3/25 to End of Semester)
    1. Web Development: Deliver a final version of the BXR website including a fully-functional QR code scanner that delivers a survey in the BXR website
